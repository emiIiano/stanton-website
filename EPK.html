STANTON

Electronic Press kit

Stanton is an indie rock band based in Castro Valley, California. Known for their raw energy, they've brought it to the stage while supporting artists including Mexican Slum Rats, Mom Cars, Wilt, Raue, Small Crush, and many more.

Brothers Ryan and Eric formed Stanton as children. Releasing their first EP "Et. Cetera" in 2019, Stanton quickly cultivated a loyal fanbase within the San Francisco Bay Area. Following their hearts, Stanton later released a fan favorite, "(Spin/Sinclair)." In June of 2025, Stanton released their latest single, "Fold," with plans to continue releasing more in Fall 2025 and Winter 2026.
